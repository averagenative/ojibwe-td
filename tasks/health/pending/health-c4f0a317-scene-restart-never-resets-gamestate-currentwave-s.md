## Finding

**Category:** MISSING_CLEANUP
**Severity:** high
**Location:** `scenes/GameScene.ts:30`

Field initializers (gameState='pregame', currentWave=0, speedMultiplier=1) run only in the constructor. When the player hits RETRY, Phaser reuses the instance and calls create() again—but create() never resets gameState, currentWave, or speedMultiplier. Because gameState remains 'over', startNextWave() returns immediately and the game is completely unplayable on retry. Fix: reset all game-state fields at the top of create().

## Finding

**Category:** MISSING_CLEANUP
**Severity:** medium
**Location:** `scenes/GameScene.ts:159`

GameScene registers input.on('pointermove'/pointerdown) and waveManager.on('wave-complete') but never defines a shutdown() override to remove them. In Phaser 3, scene.events listeners ARE auto-cleaned, but input listeners and external EventEmitter subscriptions (WaveManager extends Phaser.Events.EventEmitter, not scene.events) persist across restarts. Fix: add a shutdown() method that calls this.input.off(), this.waveManager.off(), and this.waveManager.cleanup().

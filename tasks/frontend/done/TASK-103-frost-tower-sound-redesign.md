# Frost Tower Sound Redesign — Softer Winter Wind

## Problem
The frost tower attack sound is too harsh and stands out too much from the other tower sounds. It's annoying and breaks the audio cohesion.

## Goal
Replace the current frost tower procedural sound with something more muted — like a soft winter wind or gentle whoosh. Should blend naturally with the other tower attack sounds rather than dominating them.

## Requirements
- Redesign the frost tower attack sound in the procedural audio system
- Use a softer, wind-like character (filtered noise, gentle whoosh)
- Reduce peak volume relative to other tower sounds
- Must still be audible and feel "cold" — just not piercing
- Test that it blends well alongside rock-hurler, thunder, and other tower sounds

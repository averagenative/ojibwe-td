## Finding

**Category:** STUB
**Severity:** high
**Location:** `systems/UpgradeManager.ts:294`

computeEffectiveStats() starts from defaultUpgradeStats(tower.def) and accumulates only in-run upgrade tier deltas, then tower.applyUpgradeStats() replaces the live upgStats entirely. Gear bonuses (applyGearToStats) and meta bonuses (applyTowerMetaToStats) applied to upgStats at placement time are silently erased on every buyUpgrade() or respec() call. Fix: UpgradeManager needs to store per-tower GearBonuses and meta upgrade tiers and re-apply them inside applyStatsToTower(), or GameScene must re-apply them via a post-upgrade hook.
